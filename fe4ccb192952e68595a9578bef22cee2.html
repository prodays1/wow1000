Investing for beginners. Why do we invest our money?

As strange as it may seem, many people do not invest just because they do not understand why they need investments and what benefits they can bring them.

In this article, we will talk about three good reasons reasons to start investing (and do it as soon as possible, without putting it off for a long time):

1) the First reason lies "on the surface»however, it still remains "invisible" to many people.

If we don't if we invest money, we will definitely spend it (i.e. we will lose it forever).

This is what happens to millions of people who "live from paycheck to paycheck", quickly "eating" all the money they earn. 

2) the Second reason for investing is that it protects us from harmful effects inflations.

Inflation, as we know, leads to an increase in the price of things that we use on a regular basis: food, clothing, etc.

In other words, inflation reduces the purchasing power of our money.

And investments allow us to maintain their purchasing power the ability:

  • The easiest way to do this is to place your money on a Bank Deposit.
  • Then the interest paid on the Deposit will compensate for inflation.

3) the Third reason is the possibility of creating a large capital at the expense of investing small amounts.

For instead, you should make a good habit of spending 10-20% of your salary every month on investments (buying currencies, stocks, and other financial instruments).

This will allow you to enable the effect of "compound interest", which will begin to rapidly multiply the money you have invested.

That's why I'm now with with this pleasure, I buy cheaper shares of large companies (through the acquisition of index funds):

  • after all, today there is a real "sale" of good securities;
  • which will definitely increase in price after the end of the current crisis.
