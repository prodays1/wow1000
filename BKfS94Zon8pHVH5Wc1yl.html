How to repay the loan faster (much faster). Three good tips

The loan not only empties our wallet. It wears out our nerves every day, spoils our health, and rewards us with an ulcer.

At the same time, there are a number of well-proven solutions that allow you to pay off much faster the available credit.

In this article, we will talk about what will help us get rid of credit, find prosperity and financial well-being.

3 good tips on how to pay off your loan faster:

1) Talk to your friends creditors

Call your Bank and ask if they can lower your loan rate. 

Yes, it might not work on the spur of the moment. However, " the demand is not beaten in the nose." 

If you are persistent and will keep track of financial news (including a decrease in rates of the Central Bank), sooner or later you will be transferred to a reduced interest rate. 

2) Determine the reason for what forced you to take out a loan

Once you find the root cause of your rash decision to take out a loan, you can dramatically accelerate your drive to get rid of it. 

Let's say that you took out a loan for because of the low amount of their salary. In this case, you need to think about changing your job, improving your skills, and finding additional earnings. 

Or were you forced to go head over heels in debt because you weren't prepared for an unexpected layoff or unexpected expenses? In this case, you will you need to fill your stash (i.e., your financial pillow) with money.

Or did you spend a lot of money because you didn't want to "fall in the dirt" in front of your colleagues and friends? Then adjust your money priorities — what is more important for you: to have a car "no worse than the neighbor" or to provide financial security well-being for your family? 

3) Find a supportive environment

It is very important to communicate with financially literate people who in practice know how to handle their finances correctly and invest them competently. 

I will share my example.

  • I have created such a community around him: it was the Kiyosaki club and the investors ' Club.
  • Which helped me gradually create all the types of passive income that Robert Kiyosaki wrote about in his books.

Now I live on the income from my financial capital.
